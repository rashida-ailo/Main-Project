About Us

Best Medical Care For Yourself and Your Family

PG Medical Trust Hospital is a well-established multi-speciality healthcare facility located in Nilambur, Malappuram District, Kerala. It is dedicated to providing high-quality and compassionate medical care across a broad spectrum of over 40 clinical specialties, including general medicine, surgery, orthopaedics, cardiology, nephrology, psychiatry and more. The hospital combines modern medical technology with a patient-centric approach, ensuring comprehensive treatment and wellness support for individuals and families. With an infrastructure that includes well-equipped consultation rooms, advanced diagnostic services, a fully equipped NICU, ICU beds, operation theatres, and supportive amenities, the hospital strives to offer accessible healthcare with ethical standards and transparent practices. PG Medical Trust Hospital has built a reputation for professional care delivered with empathy, making it a trusted choice for patients in Nilambur and the surrounding communities.
